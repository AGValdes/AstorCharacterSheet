Astor Qindriel

Half-Elf | 29 | Female

"She has a haunted expression, the darkness she has seen only amplifies the light she brings into the lives of those around her"

Appearance

  • Height:5'2"
  • Weight: 140lbs
  • Eyes: Golden Hazel
  • Hair:Brown, Long, Curled into ringlets or woven into braids
  • Clothing:Astor sports several pieces of armor that she has found and collected during her travels, each clearly belonging to a different armor set, but each sharing a similar theme: they are all adorned with some sort of floral decal or ornate filigree, and appear to be hand-painted gold.

Top Moral Priorities

  1. Protection: She will put herself in danger without question if it is to save her friends or family, even strangers that she finds beauty in.
  2. Pacifism: She will restrain from offensive combat in all situations except for an extreme case where it may be neccisary to save a very close loved one's life.
  3. Pursuit of Beauty:Encourage and protect beauty wherever you can find it.
  4. Belief in Love:Let warm friendship and admiration blossom where love cannot or darest not.
